Intr — Account & Data Deletion

Last updated: 16 June 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your Intr account and the personal data associated with it. It applies to the Intr mobile application published by Omar Labidi. For anything else, contact us at labidiomar04@gmail.com.

How to request deletion

You can delete your account in either of two ways:

  • In the app (fastest): open Intr, go to Profile → Delete account, type DELETE to confirm, and your account and data are removed immediately.
  • By email: send a message to labidiomar04@gmail.com with the subject "Delete my account", sent from the email address registered to your Intr account so we can verify the request. We will complete the deletion and confirm by reply.

What gets deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes all of the following:

DataExamples
Profile & onboarding dataUsername, goal, fitness level, split preference, training days
Training plan & coachingYour weekly plan and any coach messages
Workout history & exercise logsCompleted sessions, exercises, weights, reps-in-reserve, energy ratings
Body metricsBodyweight entries you logged
Daily check-ins & journal entriesReflections and any mood/energy you recorded
Account & authenticationYour email/login record in our authentication system
Timeframe. Your data is removed from our active systems immediately and is fully and permanently deleted across all systems and backups within 72 hours. Limited diagnostic logs (crash/error reports) are retained for a short period and then automatically discarded.

What is not stored by us

Subscription and purchase records are managed by the Google Play billing system and RevenueCat. Deleting your Intr account does not cancel a subscription — manage or cancel that in your Google Play account settings. We do not control billing and cannot cancel it on your behalf.

Questions

If you have any trouble deleting your account, email labidiomar04@gmail.com and we will help. See also our Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.
