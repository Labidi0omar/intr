How it works

A coach, not a calculator.

Adapts to your training

Intr tracks how your lifts, energy, and effort are trending — and adjusts your plan to match. Strong stretch, it pushes. Run down, it backs off. On purpose, never as a failure.

Explains every decision

Every weight comes with the reasoning behind it, drawn from your own numbers. You don't just follow the plan — you understand it, and learn as you lift.

No guilt, no gimmicks

Miss a week and Intr rebuilds your plan from where you actually are. No broken streaks, no badges, no manipulation. Just calm, clear training.
